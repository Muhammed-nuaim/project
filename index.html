SPACE TORNADO

Cosmic Adventure

Space Tornado are not just watches with tourbillons. This collection is a manifesto of boldness, a symbol of striving for the stars, and an invitation to possess a piece of infinity. The mesmerizing rotation of the tourbillons reminds one of the whirlwinds raging in the vast expanses of space.

TOURBILLON

The tourbillon is the pinnacle of watchmaking, a mechanism that compensates for the influence of gravity on the accuracy of the watch.

It has a shape reminiscent of a whirlwind, hence it was named "Tourbillon" (French for "whirlwind") by its inventor, the legendary watchmaker Louis Breguet, in 1795.

Tourbillon mechanisms are very complex to manufacture. Therefore, watches with a tourbillon are the most prestigious.

STARGAZER

With an authentic piece of the SpaceX Starship

Stargazer symbolizes humanity's desire for knowledge, the stars, and space. By gazing at the stars, people take the first step toward space travel, such as the flights of the Starship spacecraft. The watch symbolically combines the blue appearance of the night sky, a tourbillon at the 6 o'clock position, and an authentic piece of the SpaceX Starship at the 12 o'clock position. Stargazer pays tribute to the past, embraces the present, and looks toward the future.

SPACE TORNADO

A stellar whirlwind that evokes the Northern Lights

The airy, intricate tourbillon mechanism, which corrects gravity and resembles a whirlwind, reminded us of the extraordinary cosmic phenomenon known as a Space Tornado. Astronomers use this term to describe the giant vortices that occur on stars. They are born from the interaction between the star's plasma and magnetic field.

OBSERVE THE MOVEMENT OF THE MECHANISMS

All tourbillon mechanisms in the Space Tornado collection watches are exposed. Admire the operation of the standard, double, and flying tourbillon in these intricate inventions of horological genius.

Now you are the owner of a true masterpiece.

TWIN SPIN

with two tourbillons

In the ultra-complex mechanism of the Twin Spin model, there are two identical tourbillons, adorned with the letter "S" representing the initial of the Space Tornado collection. The special design of the Twin Spin allows you to observe the movements of the mechanism through the sapphire glass covering the dial and through the transparent back of the case. Beauty and harmony are achieved through the simplicity of the design and the synchronous rotation of the twin tourbillons.

SKYWARD

With a flying mechanism

The music of the spheres is embodied in the dial of the SkyWard watch. The skeletonized chronograph allows you to observe all the details of the watch and tourbillon mechanism. The transparent case adds airiness to the intricate design of the model. The mechanism seems to float weightlessly, like a celestial body.
